Learn More About Our Internship Program

Program Details

Our 1-month internship program is designed to provide hands-on experience in various fields of technology. Whether you are interested in data analysis, web development, Java development, Python development, or machine learning, we have a position for you.

During the internship, you will work on real-world projects, gain valuable insights from experienced professionals, and develop skills that will help you advance your career.

Benefits

  • Internship Completion Certificate
  • Eligibility to apply for stipend-based internship after completion

You have the option to pay $249 to apply for the next interview process for a stipend-based internship, or you can pay $149 and claim only the completion certificate.
